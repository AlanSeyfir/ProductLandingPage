The best ice cream contains the best ingredients

The proof of great ice cream is in the taste and our ice creams are only crafted from the very best fruit, berries, nuts and chocolate – all then mixed together with a huge helping of our very own creamy, organic milk.

Flavours

Vanilla

Our own organic milk, local double cream and Madagascan vanilla – that’s it! Perfect and always our best seller.

Strawberries & Cream

We just add strawberries, whole and pureed, and we never add any colour. Sprinkle some toasted hazelnuts on top.

Raspberries & Cream

One of the first flavours we made and still one of our best. Whole raspberries mixed with cream, does it get any better? Serve with chunks of good dark chocolate if you like.

Double Chocolate

Dark and rich, but not too sweet. High quality cocoa powder and chocolate – simple. Sprinkle with some toasted almonds.

Mango & Lime

Promise, it works. Again, all about balance. Serve with a gooey meringue.

Honeycomb

Our second most popular flavour! Can you ever have too much honeycomb people ask? Balance is key – not enough and it’s caramel, too much and it’s too sweet and soft.
